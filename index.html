Nickname: Sachio  |  Role: Support & Secondary AWPer  |  Team Liquid CS 1.6 Division
Support 2nd AWPer Indonesia Active Futsal Captain

Sachio is the glue that holds Team Liquid's CS 1.6 lineup together. As the team's Support and Secondary AWPer, he operates in the background of every round — throwing flashes at the perfect moment, smoking off angles before they become problems, and following up the entry fragger clean. His AWP is not the most aggressive on the server, but it is the most patient. He picks angles others overlook, holds them longer than is comfortable, and converts them when it matters. His flash accuracy is among the highest on the roster, and his teammates rarely take a site without his setup already in place.

Outside of Counter-Strike, Sachio is a high school student at SMAIT As-Syifa Boarding School Jalancagak, where he serves as captain of the school's futsal team. He was also part of the Acara Department during As-Syifa Festival Raya, contributing to one of the school's largest annual events. Known for his calm demeanor and team-first attitude, Sachio brings the same reliability off the server that he does on it.
